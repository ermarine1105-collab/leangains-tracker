Berkhan's Fasting Protocol

  • Fast: 16 hours (typically 8pm - 12pm next day)
  • Feed: 8 hour window (12pm - 8pm)
  • Pre-workout: Train fasted or 10g BCAAs
  • Post-workout: First meal (largest of the day)
  • Meal timing: 2-3 meals in feeding window

Berkhan's Macro Cycling Guidelines

  • KEY: All calculations based on LEAN BODY MASS, not total weight
  • Protein: 1g per lb of LBM daily (constant on all days)
  • Training days: High carb (50-60% cals), low fat (20%)
  • Rest days: Low carb (20-30% cals), higher fat (40-50%)
  • Cut (>15% BF): 14× LBM training, 10× LBM rest = ~1 lb/week loss
  • Recomp (10-15% BF): 16× LBM training, 12× LBM rest = maintenance
  • Post-workout meal: 40-50% of daily calories (break fast here)
